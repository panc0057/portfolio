COMPOSITION

One of the main categories of artistic creativity. It represents an artistic-figurative, content-formal integrity - the most complex and perfect type of structure in which all elements are organically connected. Any composition is based on some idea expressed verbally or visually, often with the help of a sketch or model

OC & CONCEPT

The original character and his concept. It includes the creation of a character and its full description: characteristics, behavior, appearance, special signs, clothing and style, jewelry, history, etc. It is an important part of the creation of stories, games, literary works and other creative creations.

ABOUT ARTIST

My name is Irina Panchenko. I am currently studying at the Interactive Media Design program at Algonquin College.

This portfolio presents my digital drawings drawn for this year. Despite the fact that drawing is not included in my program, it is a nice addition. Having started drawing in kindergarten, and then at school, I entered an art school and received a professional education and an art base there. Now I continue to study and explore digital drawing. Digital art has no boundaries, new ways and options for expressing your ideas and thoughts are constantly emerging. Therefore, I am very interested in them, and I will continue to expand my boundaries and try new things.
